Sarah's Day

Sarah is a diligent student. Every morning, she wakes up at 5:30 AM. After she gets up, she makes her bed and then she takes a shower. At 6:15 AM, she has breakfast with her family. They usually eat bread and drink milk. She goes to school by bus at 6:45 AM. Her classes start at 7:30 AM and finish at 2:00 PM. After school, she has lunch and takes a short nap. In the afternoon, she usually >does her homework. She loves reading books, so she often spends an hour reading before dinner. She has dinner at 7:00 PM. Before going to bed, she prepares her books for the next day. Finally, she goes to sleep at 9:30 PM.
