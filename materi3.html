Material 3: Telling about your daily activites

We can organise the sentences into three paragraphs:
  • Paragraphs 1: My morning activities
  • Paragraphs 2: Activities during the day
  • Paragraphs 3: Evening activities

Paragraph 1: I wake up at 7:00, brush my teeth, shower, get dressed, eat breakfast, and prepare lunch. Then I drive to work.

Paragraph 2: At 8:30, I start work, check emails, and use the computer. I have lunch at 1:00 with co-workers, then work until 5:30 before heading home.

Paragraph 3: After work, I go running or read. I usually make a salad for dinner but have pizza on Fridays. Then I watch TV and go to bed.
