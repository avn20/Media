Material 2: Languange Usage

1. Write Present Simple Sentences Use the present simple to describe routines or habits. Example: "I brush my teeth. I have coffee for breakfast."

2. Use Adverbs of Frequency Add adverbs like "usually" or "sometimes" to improve your sentences. Example: "I usually have coffee, but sometimes I skip breakfast."

3. Use Connectors Use words like and, but, so to join sentences. Example: "I get up at 7:30 and brush my teeth."

4. Use Time Expressions Incorporate time words to show sequence. Example: "I get up at 7:00. After that, I shower. Then, I have breakfast."
